Energy Clicker: Light up world

Develop your energy generation skills.
A game where every click brings you more light.

Enter the captivating world of Energy Clicker: Light up world and become a true master of electricity generation! You will take control of the main character who, by pressing the generator pedal, unleashes immense power and lights up the windows of the city. The faster you tap the screen, the more energy you generate and illuminate more houses. However, be careful because if the energy exceeds the permissible value, your generator will start to break down!

In Energy Clicker: Light up world, you have the opportunity to upgrade the strength, stamina, and intelligence of your character. Strength allows you to press the pedal faster, thereby generating more energy. Stamina plays an important role, if it runs out, your hero starts pressing the pedal more slowly, slowing down the energy generation process. Intelligence helps you quickly repair and improve the generators to make them more efficient.

In Energy Clicker: Light up world, you can acquire more powerful generators, strength, stamina and intelligence boosters, as well as change the skins of your character. Immerse yourself in the thrilling energy race and strive for perfection to unlock the next city. Numerous levels await you, each requiring more power and skills to light up all the windows in the city.

Become a leader in electricity generation and show everyone your energetic side in Energy Clicker: Light up world!
