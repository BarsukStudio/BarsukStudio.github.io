Zoom Diving: Picture Chain

Relax with an infinite-zoom puzzle. Explore every picture within a picture.

Zoom Diving: Picture Chain is a relaxing zoom game with a unique picture-in-picture mechanic, where each image hides the next. Zoom in, find the hidden picture, and keep going — no hidden objects, no timers, just pure exploration at your own pace.
Dive into a never-ending visual journey and discover where each picture leads next!
