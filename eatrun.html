Eat and Run Clicker

Tap to train and run, devour food, and smash records to become the champion


Do you love a good challenge and delicious food? Dive into the ultimate clicker game, Eat and Run Clicker, where you balance intense workouts and indulgent meals to become the ultimate champion! The faster you tap, the faster your will train, run, and eat food, making every tap count towards your success.

Game Features:

Train, Eat, Compete!
• Gym Workouts: Burn calories on the exercise bike. The fitter you are, the faster you pedal! • Cafe Feasts: Indulge in a variety of delicious foods to gain weight and experience. The heavier you are, the easier it is to eat more! • Running Competitions: Test your running endurance and set new records. • Eating Challenges: Prove your eating prowess by devouring mountains of food. • Fatter and thinner: Observe the changes in the body in real time.

Level Up & Customize
• Earn Experience: Gain experience from both training and eating to level up your character. • Boost Your Stats: Allocate points to enhance your Strength, Endurance, and Metabolism.

Earn Big & Spend Wisely
• Set Records & Earn Rewards: Compete in running and eating challenges to win big money! • Invest in Yourself: Use your earnings to buy delicious food, intense training sessions, and powerful boosts.

Eat and Run Clicker offers a unique blend of fitness, delicious food, and competition. Whether you're working out in the gym, enjoying meals in the cafe, or competing in thrilling events, there's always a new goal to reach. Embark on your journey to become the ultimate fit foodie!
